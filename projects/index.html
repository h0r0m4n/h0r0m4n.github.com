Projects

A glimpse into my career journey reveals a diverse spectrum of projects I've had the privilege to engage with.

These undertakings span various industries and creative challenges:

  1. Place

    Valentino

    I spearheaded the restyling of Valentino's intranet portal, a vital platform utilized by Valentino employees. Key functionalities encompassed calendar management, company news dissemination, social and magazine streams, and access to online courses.

    • Web
    • 2020

  2. Main Website

    IED

    I contributed to the restyling of the main website for IED, a renowned design institute with locations in Italy, Spain, Brazil, and China, serving over 100,000 students. The updated site is scheduled for publication in 2021.

    • Web
    • 2020

  3. Data Visualization

    UBI Banca

    I led the design of a visual language for Data Visualization widgets used in UBI Banca’s Data Intelligence internal product.

    • Web
    • 2019

  4. Data Visualization

    • Web
    • 2019

    I had the honor of collaborating on the restyling of Ferrari's Media Center website. As a globally distributed platform, it caters to journalists, writers, publishers, and editors, offering exclusive content that embodies Ferrari's brand philosophy of originality, aspiration, and inspiration.

    • Web
    • 2018

  6. Meu TIM

    TIM

    I collaborated with TIM, Italy's largest telecommunications service provider with an annual turnover of over 18 billion euros, to restyle the MyTIM app for Brazil.
